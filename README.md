# Typing Speed Battle

## Game Overview
Typing Speed Battle is an engaging multiplayer game that tests players' typing skills in real-time. Players compete against each other to see who can type a given passage of text the fastest.

## Features
- **Real-time Competition**: Compete with friends or random opponents in real-time.
- **Multiple Difficulty Levels**: Choose from different text passages of varying lengths and complexities.
- **Leaderboard**: Track your progress and see how you rank against other players.
- **User-friendly Interface**: Simple and intuitive design for players of all ages.

## Instructions
1. **Start the Game**: After loading the game, click on "Start" to enter the lobby.
2. **Choose Opponent**: You can either invite friends or join a random game.
3. **Typing Challenge**: Once the game starts, you will see a passage of text. Type it as fast and accurately as possible.
4. **Finish Line**: The winner is the first player to complete the text correctly. 
5. **Review Scores**: After the game, your typing speed, accuracy, and rank will be displayed.
